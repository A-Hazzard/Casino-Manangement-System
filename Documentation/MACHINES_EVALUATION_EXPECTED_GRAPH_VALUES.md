## Notes

- All percentages are rounded to 1 decimal place in the UI
- The Summary section shows Pareto principle statements (what % of machines contribute to what % of total)
- Charts show percentage contributions relative to totals
- Floor Positions % is calculated as: (machines with manufacturer/game / total machines) × 100
- All other percentages use Calc. 3: (manufacturer/game metric / total metric) × 100
